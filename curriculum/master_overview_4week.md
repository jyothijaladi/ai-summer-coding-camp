# 🗓️ Master Curriculum Overview: 20-Day AI Coding Journey

**Schedule:** 9:00 AM - 12:00 PM (Monday - Friday)
**Rhythm:** 2 Concepts/day (40m each) + 20m Break + 60m Sandbox/Exploration

---

## 📅 Week 1: The Logic Architect (The Foundation)
*Focus: How computers think and store information.*

- **Day 1:** 👋 **Hello World & 📦 Variable Boxes**
    - The "Magic" of printing and storing numbers.
- **Day 2:** 🔢 **The Math Lab & ⚖️ Comparing Numbers**
    - Basic calculations and learning "Greater than / Less than."
- **Day 3:** 📝 **The Word Wizard (Strings) & ✨ Formatting**
    - Mastery of text, quotes, and f-strings.
- **Day 4:** 💬 **The Conversation (Input) & 🚦 If/Else Logic**
    - Making code interactive and reactive.
- **Day 5:** 🏁 **Concept: Booleans & Type Checking** + 🏆 **HERO PROJECT:** **The AI Escape Room**
    - Completing the data types and building a branching text-adventure.

---

## 📅 Week 2: The Loop Master (Handling Data)
*Focus: Pattern recognition and working with groups of items.*

- **Day 6:** 🔗 **Comparison & 🧠 Logical Operators**
    - Combining conditions using `and`, `or`, and `not`.
- **Day 7:** 🛍️ **Lists & 🧊 Tuples (The "Frozen" List)**
    - Storing collections and learning about "Unchangeable" data.
- **Day 8:** 🔁 **For Loops & 📜 Iteration**
    - Automating repetitive tasks across lists.
- **Day 9:** 🔄 **While Loops & 🛑 Break Statements**
    - Running code until a specific goal is met.
- **Day 10:** 🌀 **Concept: Nested Loops & Grids** + 🏆 **HERO PROJECT:** **The Virtual AI Pet**
    - Building complex patterns and a smart pet with memory.

---

## 📅 Week 3: The System Designer (Functions & Organization)
*Focus: Building reusable machines and organized data.*

- **Day 11:** 📖 **Dictionaries & 💎 Sets (Unique Collections)**
    - Organizing data with keys and managing "unique" items.
- **Day 12:** 📦 **Intro to Functions & 🏗️ Defining Logic**
    - Creating reusable blocks of code (`def`).
- **Day 13:** 📥 **Parameters & 📤 Return Values**
    - Passing information into and getting answers out of functions.
- **Day 14:** 💂 **Scope (Global vs Local) & 🎲 The Random Library**
    - Managing variables across functions and adding "Luck" to games.
- **Day 15:** 🐛 **Concept: Error Handling (Try/Except)** + 🏆 **HERO PROJECT:** **The Boss Battle RPG**
    - Building a combat system with health points, special moves (functions), and a boss stat-sheet (dictionary).

---

## 📅 Week 4: The Innovation Lab (The Project Marathon)
*Focus: Mastering digital memory and building one mini-app every day.*

- **Day 16:** 💾 **Digital Memory (Reading & Writing Files)**
    - Learn to save data permanently and load it back later.
- **Day 17:** 🏰 **PROJECT 1: The Haunted House Builder**
    - Create a map-making game where you "save" your dungeon to a file.
- **Day 18:** ⛏️ **PROJECT 2: The Minecraft Inventory Clone**
    - Saving and loading complex inventories (JSON) that never reset.
- **Day 19:** 🕵️ **PROJECT 3: The AI Detective Agency**
    - Use AI to generate clues and store your "Case Files" in a folder.
- **Day 20:** 🏆 **GRAND FINALE: The Infinite Adventure Book**
    - An AI-narrated story that saves your unique choices to a final "Tale" file.
