## 🕒 9:00 AM – 9:40 AM: Concept – Booleans & Type Checking 🏁
*The computer's ultimate "Yes" or "No".*

1.  **What is a Boolean?**
    - Explain: `True` or `False`. It’s like a light switch.
    - Note: Capital letters matter! `true` (lowercase) will cause an error.
2.  **Checking Types (`type()`):**
    - Sometimes we forget what's in a box. Use `print(type(variable))` to see if it's a string, int, or float.
3.  **The AI Connection:**
    - Ask Gemini: "What is the difference between equal `=` and double equal `==` in Python? Explain it to me like I'm 10."

## 🕒 10:00 AM – 12:00 PM: 🏆 FRIDAY HERO PROJECT: The AI Escape Room 🏰
*Goal: Build a game where the player must solve riddles and logic gates to escape a spooky room.*

### The Project Blueprint:
1.  **The Welcome:** Use `print` and `input` to welcome the player and get their name.
2.  **Gate 1 (The Door):** Use an `if/else` statement to ask for a secret password.
3.  **Gate 2 (The Math Lock):** Ask the user to solve a math problem (multiplication or addition). Compare their answer to the correct number.
4.  **Gate 3 (The Final Boss):** Ask a riddle. Use `if/else` to check the answer.
5.  **The Victory:** If they pass all three, print a custom message using their name and an f-string.

### The AI Advantage:
- **Stuck on a Riddle?** Ask Gemini: "Give me a simple riddle that has a one-word answer for my Python game."
- **Code not working?** Ask Gemini: "I have an error in my `if` statement. Can you look at this code and tell me what's wrong?"
