## 🕒 10:00 AM – 10:40 AM: Concept 2 – f-Strings (The Ultimate Sentence Maker) ✨
*The professional way to put variables inside text.*

1.  **The Problem:** `"Hello " + name + ", your score is " + str(score)` is messy.
2.  **The Solution (f-Strings):**
    - Show them the "f-bracket" magic: `f"Hello {name}, your score is {score}"`.
    - Explain that the curly brackets `{}` are like holes in the sentence where the box contents fall in.
3.  **The AI Connection:**
    - Task: Ask Gemini: "Convert this messy code into a clean f-string: `print('The ' + animal + ' jumped over the ' + object)`."

## 🕒 11:00 AM – 12:00 PM: 🏝️ The Sandbox Exploration (Level 3)
*Goal: Create an "AI Mad Libs" Intro.*

**Challenges for the Students:**

3.  **The AI Boss Fight:**

---

### 💡 Teacher's Tip:
*Watch out for students forgetting the 'f' before the opening quote. It’s the #1 mistake with f-strings! If the output shows the curly brackets `{}` instead of the variable, it means they forgot the 'f'.*
