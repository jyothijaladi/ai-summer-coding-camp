# 🛠️ Concept 1 Practice: The Megaphone Masterclass

### 🚀 The 20-Problem Challenge Menu
*Teachers: Let students choose any 5 to 10 missions based on their interest!*

#### 🟢 Group A: The Copy-Cat Warmups
1.  **First Words:** Print "Hello World!" exactly.
2.  **Double Talk:** Print two separate messages on two lines.
3.  **The Greeting:** Print "Welcome to the AI Summer Camp!"
4.  **Emoji Power:** Print your three favorite emojis in a row.
5.  **Triple Threat:** Use three separate `print()` commands to tell a story about a cat.

#### 🟡 Group B: The Bug Squasher (Fix the Errors)
6.  **Missing Megaphone:** `print "Hey there"` (Fix it!).
7.  **Unwrapped Gift:** `print(Hello)` (Fix the missing quotes!).
8.  **One-Sided:** `print("Goodbye)` (Fix the missing closing quote!).
9.  **Capital Offense:** `Print("Testing...")` (Fix the capital 'P'!).
10. **The Scrambler:** `(print)Hello"` (Fix the order!).

#### 🔴 Group C: The Creative Builder (Logic & Design)
11. **The Border:** Use `print` to draw a line of 20 dashes (`-`).
12. **The Signature:** Print your name inside a box made of asterisks (`*`).
13. **The Pyramid:** Use `print` to draw a small triangle using the `/` and `\` symbols.
14. **The Ghost:** Print "BOO!" five times in a row manually.
15. **The Countdown:** Print 3, 2, 1 on separate lines.

#### 💎 Group D: The AI Whisperer (Gemini Missions)
16. **The ASCII Artist:** Ask Gemini to generate a `print` command for a simple robot face.
17. **The Riddle:** Ask Gemini for a riddle and use `print` to show the riddle and then the answer on a new line.
18. **The Poet:** Ask Gemini for a 2-line poem about Python and print it.
19. **The Coder's Quote:** Ask Gemini for a famous quote about technology and print it with the author's name.
20. **The Secret Mission:** Ask Gemini: "Give me one secret Python `print` trick that I haven't learned yet!"

---

### 🌟 Stretch Goal for "Flash" Students
Can you create a **Full Page Border** using only `print` commands?
*Challenge:* Try to create a "Robot Receipt" that shows a list of items and a "Total" at the bottom!
