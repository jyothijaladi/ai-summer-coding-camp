## 1. The Core Concept: Designing Before Coding 🗺️

Professional programmers do not just sit down and start typing. They design the blueprint first!
For an **Agentic AI Project**, our blueprint needs three main parts:
1. **The Goal:** What does the app actually do? (e.g. A Pokemon recommender)
2. **The Tools:** What Python functions will the AI need to use? (e.g. `search_pokedex(type)`, `calculate_damage(move)`)
3. **The Persona (System Prompt):** Who is the AI acting like? (e.g. Professor Oak)

---

## 2. Kid Q&A: "Why is this useful?" 🤔

**Q: Do I really have to plan it out? Can't I just build it?**
**A:** Imagine trying to build a Lego Star Destroyer without the instruction booklet. If you just grab bricks randomly, you will end up with a weird square block instead of a spaceship. A good plan means when you get stuck, you can look at the blueprint to remember what you are supposed to be building!
