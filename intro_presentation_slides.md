# 📝 Welcome to AI Code Camp! (Presentation Slides)

*Teacher Note: Write these points on a whiteboard, or project them on a screen. Go through them slowly and have the kids interact!*

---

## SLIDE 1: Welcome to the Future! 🚀

**Title:** What are we doing here?

**Points:**
*   **Coding isn't typing... it's teaching!** We are here to teach the computer how to play our games and do our chores.
*   **The super weapon: AI.** You aren't learning alone. You are getting a Robot Partner named Gemini.
*   **The Goal:** Build an app that can think for itself!

**Activity:** Have the class shout out their favorite app or game. (Roblox? TikTok? Minecraft?) Remind them: *Every single pixel on that screen was created by code.*

---

## SLIDE 2: The Three Ingredients 🎂

**Title:** Our Toolbox

**Points:**
1.  🐍 **Python:** The language we use to talk to the computer. It's much easier than learning French or Spanish because it just uses English words like `if` and `print`!
2.  💻 **VS Code:** Our workbench. This is the app where we write our spells.
3.  🧠 **Gemini (LLM):** Our AI brain. If we get stuck, we ask our brain in the jar for help!

**Questions to ask:** "Why do you think the python logo is two snakes?" (Answer: It’s not actually named after the snake, it was named after a comedy TV show called Monty Python! Programmers love jokes!)

---

## SLIDE 3: What is "Agentic AI?" 🤖

**Title:** You are building Iron Man's JARVIS.

**Points:**
*   **Old AI:** You ask a question, it gives an answer. (Like a smart encyclopedia).
*   **Agentic AI:** You give it a GOAL, and it uses TOOLS to achieve the goal by itself.

**Analogy:** 
*Normal AI:* "How do I build a house?" -> *AI gives you a list of instructions.*
*Agentic AI:* "Build me a house." -> *AI orders wood from the internet, talks to a robot builder, and builds the house.*

We are going to give our AI the ability to actually *press buttons* on our computer!
