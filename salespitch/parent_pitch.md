## 📅 The 4-Week Journey

### Week 1: The Logic Architect 🧩
Students master variables and logical thinking. 
**Hero Project:** Build an "AI Escape Room" text game.

### Week 2: The Loop Master 🔁
Learning how to handle data and repetition—the core of all apps.
**Hero Project:** Create a "Virtual AI Pet" with its own personality and memory.

### Week 3: The System Designer ⚙️
Breaking complex problems into "Functions" (Mini-machines).
**Hero Project:** The "Secret Agent Portal" – A multi-tool security app.

### Week 4: The Innovation Lab 🚀
Building real-world web applications and AI assistants.
**Hero Project:** **THE DREAM APP.** Students design and deploy their own custom AI tool to show off to friends and family!
